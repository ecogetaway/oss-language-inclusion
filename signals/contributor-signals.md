# Contributor Signals

This file captures contributor-side **patterns** from public i18n participation threads and PR workflows referenced in this repository. It focuses on the contributor experience of proposing, updating, and maintaining language changes.

## Observed Patterns

- Lack of structured workflow for i18n contributions
- Review delays due to maintainer bandwidth
- Difficulty maintaining consistency across large numbers of strings
- Limited recognition compared to code contributions

## Example Inputs

- “Review process is unclear and slow”
- “No structured way to maintain translations over time”
