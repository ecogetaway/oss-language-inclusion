# Maintainer Feedback

This folder collects perspectives from open-source maintainers on the 
challenges of supporting language inclusion contributions (i18n, l10n, 
translation PRs) in their repositories.

## How to contribute

If you are a maintainer and have experienced challenges with i18n contributions
— due to bandwidth, tooling, workflow, or other constraints — we would value
your perspective.

Open an issue or submit a PR adding a brief note here. 
Even 2-3 sentences helps establish the pattern.

## Why this matters

Maintainer perspectives are the primary evidence base for our case that 
language inclusion needs structured infrastructure — not just volunteer effort.
